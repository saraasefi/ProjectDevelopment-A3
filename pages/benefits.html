Benefits of yoga

Roll out your yoga mat to benefit from physical and mental exercises that have hooked yoga practitioners all over the world for many years. you do not need to be a yogi to benefit from yoga exercises and this is the magic of Yoga! Yoga is for everyone; whether you are young or old, fit or not fit, have a flexible body or not, yoga can relax and strength you mind and body at the same time.

1. Yoga improves strength, balance and flexibility
2. Yoga helps with back pain relief
3. Yoga can ease arthritis symptoms
4. Yoga benefits heart health
5. Yoga relaxes you, to help you sleep better
6. Yoga can mean more energy and brighter moods
7. Yoga helps you manage stress
8. Yoga connects you with a supportive community
9. Yoga promotes better self-care
